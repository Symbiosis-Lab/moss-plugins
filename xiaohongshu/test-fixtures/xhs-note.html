Spring in Kyoto is absolutely magical! The cherry blossoms are in full bloom and every street corner is a photo opportunity.

Day 1: Visited Kiyomizu-dera temple. The cherry blossoms framing the temple were breathtaking. Got there early at 6am to avoid crowds.

Day 2: Walked the Path of Philosophy. The canal lined with sakura trees is the most peaceful walk you'll ever take in Kyoto.

Day 3: Fushimi Inari at sunset. The torii gates look incredible with the golden light filtering through.

Tips: Book accommodation 3 months ahead. Cherry blossom season is late March to mid April.
